Refund Policy

Last updated: 1 March 2026

We want every Art & Beyond experience to be wonderful. Here's our straightforward refund policy.

Workshop Cancellations by You

  • Cancellation 48+ hours before: Full refund within 5–7 business days to original payment method.
  • Cancellation under 48 hours: No cash refund, but you receive a free reschedule credit valid for 3 months — use it for any upcoming Art & Beyond event.
  • No-show on the day: Non-refundable. Reschedule credits are not issued for no-shows.

Workshop Cancellations by Art & Beyond

In the unlikely event we cancel a workshop (due to low attendance, venue issues, or force majeure), you receive:

  • A full refund to your original payment method, or
  • A free reschedule to the next available event of your choice — plus a ₹200 loyalty credit for the inconvenience.

Gift Cards

Gift cards are non-refundable but never expire. They can be used for any Art & Beyond workshop or private event.

How to Request a Refund

  1. Email hello@artandbeyond.in with subject: Refund Request — [Your Booking Ref]
  2. Or WhatsApp us at +91 70572 57021 with your booking reference.
  3. We'll acknowledge your request within 4 hours (10am–8pm IST, Mon–Sun).
  4. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days via the original payment method.
