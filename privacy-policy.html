Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 March 2026

Art & Beyond ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website artandbeyond.in. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you interact with us:

  • Booking information: Name, email address, phone number, number of guests, and payment reference number.
  • Newsletter subscriptions: Email address.
  • Reviews: Name, workshop name, review text, and optional photo.
  • Automatically collected: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time of visit via standard server logs.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To confirm and manage your workshop bookings.
  • To send event updates and workshop notifications (only if you opted in).
  • To display approved reviews on our website.
  • To improve our website and services.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Supabase (database hosting) — data stored on servers within the EU/US under their data processing agreement.
  • WhatsApp (Meta Platforms) — only when you initiate a booking confirmation via WhatsApp.

4. Data Retention

Booking data is retained for 3 years for accounting and legal compliance. Newsletter subscriptions are retained until you unsubscribe. You may request deletion at any time by emailing us.

5. Your Rights

Under applicable law (including India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.

6. Cookies

We use no tracking or advertising cookies. Our website uses only essential functionality. Your browser may set session cookies required for normal operation.
